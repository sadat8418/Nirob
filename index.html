About

NIROB (Nirapod Rokter Bondhon), a nonprofit organization, was founded on September 1st, 2013, by Md. Ruhul Amin Rashed and a few students. This organization began its journey at Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology(RUET) with the goal of ensuring safe blood transfusion. It has now become a large family in which volunteers join to manage or donate blood for the people in need. Blood is essential to saving lives, whether in an accident or during surgery. Even patients have passed away because to a donor scarcity, as we have seen.
Even our loved ones might need blood at any time. As blood cells renew themselves inside, a person can donate blood just once every three months. Blood donation lowers the risk of developing ischaemic heart disease because it removes the body's undesirable and accumulated iron load. Why not contribute something that can save a person's life, is wasted every three months, and costs nothing?
Anyone can become a savoir. Bring a life back to power. Make blood donation your responsibility. Donate Blood! Save Lives!
